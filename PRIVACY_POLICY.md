# Privacy Policy — Daniel Fast

**Last updated: 2 June 2026**

Daniel Fast ("the app", "we", "us") is a personal fasting, hydration and devotional
tracker. This policy explains what data the app handles. The short version: **the app
keeps everything on your device and does not collect, transmit, or share your personal
data.**

## Information we collect

**None that leaves your device.**

The app lets you record things like your fast start/end times, water and Rooibos
counts, chosen fast length, completed days, and reading-plan progress. All of this is
stored **locally on your device** using the device's local storage. It is never sent to
us or to any third party, and we have no servers that receive it.

We do **not** collect:

- Personal identifiers (name, email, phone, account, advertising ID)
- Location data
- Health or fitness data sent off the device
- Usage analytics or crash reports
- Contacts, photos, files, or messages

## Permissions the app uses

- **Notifications** (`POST_NOTIFICATIONS`, Android 13+): used only to show the
  reminders you choose to enable (water, Rooibos tea, fast start/complete). You can
  turn these off at any time in the app or in Android settings. We do not use
  notifications to collect any information.
- **Receive boot completed / wake lock**: used by the notification system to keep your
  chosen reminders scheduled after the device restarts. No data is collected.

## Links to other services

The app contains links to Bible.com (YouVersion) for reading scripture. When you tap a
link, your device's browser opens that website. Those sites have their own privacy
policies, which we do not control. The app shares no data with them — it only opens a
public web page.

## Children's privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any personal data from anyone, including children.

## Data deletion

Because your data lives only on your device, you can delete all of it at any time by:

- Using the in-app reset/clear controls (History → Clear History; Bible → Reset; Settings → Change Fast Length), or
- Clearing the app's storage in Android Settings → Apps → Daniel Fast → Storage → Clear data, or
- Uninstalling the app.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above and post the new
version at the same URL.
