Privacy Policy

Last updated: 21 June 2026

Daniel Fast is a simple fasting, hydration, Rooibos and devotional tracker. This privacy policy explains what information the app uses and how it is handled.

Information We Collect

The app is designed for personal tracking. It may store fasting times, daily progress, hydration counts, Rooibos counts, devotional progress and related app settings on your device.

How Information Is Used

Your tracking information is used only to show your own progress inside the app, keep your history available, and support the app's fasting and wellness features.

Local Storage

Daniel Fast stores app data locally on your device. We do not sell your data and we do not use your personal tracking data for advertising.

Third-Party Services

The app may be distributed through Google Play, which may collect information according to Google's own policies. If the app is hosted or viewed on the web, the hosting provider may process basic technical information such as IP address, browser type and access logs.

Health And Wellness Disclaimer

Daniel Fast is for general wellness and spiritual tracking only. It is not medical advice, does not diagnose or treat any condition, and is not a medical device. Speak to a qualified healthcare professional before fasting if you are pregnant, diabetic, under 18, taking medication, or managing a medical condition.

Children

The app is not intended for children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can help address it.

Data Deletion

You can clear app data from your device by using your device's app storage settings or uninstalling the app.

Changes To This Policy

We may update this policy when the app changes. The updated date at the top of this page will show the latest version.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the app owner through the support details provided in the Google Play listing.
